The goal of the Privacy-Preserving Analytics in Medicine (PrivateAIM) project is to develop a federated platform for privacy-preserving machine learning and data analytics for the Medical Informatics Initiative (MII). For this, customized AI methods, federation mechanisms, and protection models will be developed to protect patients' identities and sensitive health information.


An overview of further use cases and projects of the MII can be found at the MII-website.
